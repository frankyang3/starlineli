About Us

Starline is a family run seafood importer specializing in both dried and frozen Seafood. Starline Li Inc. has been seafood importer since 2012. We supply a variety of high-quality importer seafood in Canada and maintain quality control at every stage. We strictly follow the regulations of Canada Food Inspection Agency (CFIA), including the Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point (HACCP) system, and achieve the standards of CFIA and CE.

Founded on the fundemental belief that quality seafood should be assesible to everyone. Starline Li ever since has been striving to bring affordable and quality seafood into the market. By cutting out the middle man and grouping up orders from costumers into one big order, we are able to bring costs down to a minimum. To learn more about how we perform our orders check out our product page.

Our Values

Quality

We eat what we sell. We believe that our costumers deserves only what we are personally happy to consume. All products that we sell have been personally tested and approved by us.

Safety

We strictly follow the regulations of Canada Food Inspection Agency (CFIA), including the Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point (HACCP) system, and achieve the standards of CFIA and CE. We always try our best to ensure the product we provide is safe to consume.

Affordability

After ensuring the quality and safety of our product, cost is our next concern. We make sure you get the most out of your hard-earned money by cutting out the middle-man and taking orders in blocks in order to sell at low prices.
