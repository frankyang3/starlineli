About Us

Starline is a family run seafood importer specializing in both dried and frozen Seafood. Starline Li Inc. has been seafood importer since 2012. We supply a variety of high-quality importer seafood in Canada and maintain quality control at every stage. We strictly follow the regulations of Canada Food Inspection Agency (CFIA), including the Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point (HACCP) system, and achieve the standards of CFIA and CE.
